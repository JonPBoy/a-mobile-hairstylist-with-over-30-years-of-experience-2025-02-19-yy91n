About Us

Founded in 1994, our mobile hair styling service began with a simple vision: to bring professional hair care directly to our clients. What started as a small, local operation has grown into a trusted name in premium mobile hair services, serving countless satisfied clients throughout Southern California.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional hair care with unmatched convenience and personalized attention. We believe in creating lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and exceeding their expectations with every visit. Our core values of professionalism, integrity, and client satisfaction drive everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide mobile hair services throughout Orange County and select areas of Los Angeles County. Our service radius includes Newport Beach, Irvine, Costa Mesa, Laguna Beach, and surrounding communities.

How long does an appointment typically take?

Hair extension services typically take 2-4 hours, while hairpiece maintenance usually requires 1-2 hours. We'll provide a specific time estimate based on your service needs during booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, Venmo, and PayPal. Payment is required at the time of service. We also offer convenient payment plans for extension services.

What products do you use?

We use only premium, professional-grade products and tools. All hair extensions are 100% human hair, and our hairpieces are sourced from top manufacturers known for quality and durability.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least one week in advance to ensure your preferred time slot. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute appointments when possible.
