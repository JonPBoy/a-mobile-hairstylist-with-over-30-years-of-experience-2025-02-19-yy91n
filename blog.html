The Complete Guide to Hair Extensions: Types, Care, and Maintenance

Discover everything you need to know about hair extensions from a seasoned professional with over 30 years of experience. This comprehensive guide covers the three main types: tape-in extensions for fine to medium hair, fusion/keratin bonds for natural movement, and micro-link/bead extensions for thick hair. Learn essential maintenance tips, including proper brushing techniques and product selection. Understanding the importance of professional installation, daily care routines, and common mistakes to avoid will help maximize your investment. Whether you're new to extensions or a longtime wearer, this guide ensures you make informed decisions for beautiful, long-lasting results.
